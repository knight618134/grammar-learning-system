## Learning Goal
EN: Use **will** for decisions, promises, offers, and predictions.

中：用 **will** 表達臨時決定、承諾、提供幫助和預測。

## Core Grammar
### A. Instant Decision / 臨時決定
EN: Use will when deciding now.

中：當下決定常用 will。

### B. Promise/Offer / 承諾/提供
EN: Will can make promises or offers.

中：will 可用於承諾或提供幫忙。

### C. Prediction / 預測
EN: Will can predict what you think will happen.

中：will 可表示你認為會發生的事。

## Examples
- I will fix that mistake.
- I will review it tonight.
- This system will help Jason learn.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Will is not only future; it can be a decision now.
- 中：will 不只是未來，也常是當下決定。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I will to review.
- Correct: I will review.

## One-line Summary
EN: Will = decision, promise, offer, prediction.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
