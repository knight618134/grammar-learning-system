## Learning Goal
EN: Use **wish** patterns for present regrets, past regrets, and complaints about repeated behavior.

中：學會 wish 的三種核心用法：現在不滿、過去後悔、希望別人/事情改變。

## Core Grammar
### A. Wish + Past / 希望現在不同
EN: Use **wish + past** when the real present situation is not what you want.

中：用 **wish + 過去式** 表達「現在不是這樣，但我希望是這樣」。

- I wish I understood this rule better.
- I wish I had more time.

### B. Wish + Past Perfect / 後悔過去
EN: Use **wish + had + past participle** when you regret something in the past.

中：用 **wish + had + 過去分詞** 表達「早知道就好了」。

- I wish I had reviewed the examples first.
- I wish I had not rushed.

### C. Wish + Would / 希望改變
EN: Use **wish + would** when you want someone or something to change. It often sounds annoyed.

中：用 **wish + would** 表達希望某人或某事改變，常帶一點抱怨。

- I wish I wouldn't rush when I answer.
- I wish this app would remind me to review.

### D. Wish vs Hope / wish 和 hope
EN: Use **hope** for possible future results. Use **wish** for unreal or regretful situations.

中：**hope** 是未來有可能；**wish** 常常是現在不是真的或過去後悔。

- I hope I pass the test.
- I wish I had passed the test.

## Examples
- I wish I understood this rule better.
- I wish I had noticed the passive form earlier.
- I wish I wouldn't rush when I answer grammar questions.
- I hope I can use this correctly next time.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- EN: **Wish** is different from **hope**.
- 中：**wish** 和 **hope** 不要混用。
- EN: **Wish + would** usually expresses annoyance or desire for change.
- 中：**wish + would** 常常表示「拜託改一下」的感覺。
- EN: If you are talking about a possible future goal, use **hope**.
- 中：如果是未來可能達成的目標，用 **hope**。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I wish I will improve soon.
- Better: I hope I will improve soon.
- Wrong: I wish I studied yesterday.
- Better: I wish I had studied yesterday.
- Wrong: I hope I had more time now.
- Better: I wish I had more time now.

## One-line Summary
EN: Use **wish** for unreal or regretful situations, and **hope** for possible future outcomes.

中：不真實或後悔用 **wish**；未來可能發生用 **hope**。
