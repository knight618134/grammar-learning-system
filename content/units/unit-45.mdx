## Learning Goal
EN: Use reporting passive patterns such as **it is said that**, **is said to**, and **is supposed to**.

中：學會用報導式被動：**it is said that**、**is said to**、**is supposed to**。

## Core Grammar
### A. It Is Said That / 據說
EN: Use **It is said that + clause** when reporting what people say.

中：用 **It is said that + 子句** 表達「據說...」。

- It is said that this pattern is common.
- It is believed that the answer is correct.

### B. Subject + Is Said To / 據說某人...
EN: Use **subject + is said to + base verb** when the reported action is current or generally true.

中：用 **主詞 + is said to + 原形動詞** 表達現在或一般性的據說。

- He is said to study every day.
- This rule is said to be useful.

### C. Subject + Is Said To Have Done / 據說已經...
EN: Use **to have + past participle** when the reported action happened earlier.

中：如果據說的動作發生在更早之前，用 **to have + 過去分詞**。

- He is said to have passed the test.
- The sentence is said to have been corrected.

### D. Be Supposed To / 應該、被認為應該
EN: **Be supposed to** can mean something is expected, arranged, or believed.

中：**be supposed to** 可以表示應該、預期、安排上應該。

- Jason is supposed to review Unit 43 today.

## Examples
- It is said that passive is common in formal English.
- This structure is said to be useful.
- The mistake is said to have been corrected.
- Jason is supposed to save important explanations.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- EN: **He is said that...** is wrong. Use **It is said that...** or **He is said to...**.
- 中：不能說 **He is said that...**。要用 **It is said that...** 或 **He is said to...**。
- EN: Earlier action uses **to have done**.
- 中：更早發生的動作用 **to have done**。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: He is said that he studies hard.
- Correct: It is said that he studies hard.
- Correct: He is said to study hard.
- Wrong: He is said to left the company.
- Correct: He is said to have left the company.

## One-line Summary
EN: Use **It is said that...** or **is said to...**, and use **to have done** for earlier actions.

中：據說句型用 **It is said that... / is said to...**；更早的動作用 **to have done**。
