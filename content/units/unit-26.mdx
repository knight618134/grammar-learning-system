title: can, could and (be) able to
---

## Learning Goal
EN: Review ability and possibility with **can**, **could**, and **be able to**.

中：複習用 **can / could / be able to** 表達能力、可能性，以及某次成功做到。

## Core Grammar
### A. Can / 現在能力
EN: Use **can + base verb** for present ability or possibility.

中：用 **can + 原形動詞** 表達現在會不會、能不能。

- I can understand this example.
- This answer can be corrected.

### B. Could / 過去一般能力
EN: Use **could** for general ability in the past.

中：用 **could** 表達過去一般能力，不一定是某一次成功。

- I could read simple English when I was younger.

### C. Was/Were Able To / 某次成功做到
EN: Use **was/were able to** for a specific successful action in the past.

中：如果是過去某一次真的成功做到，常用 **was/were able to**。

- I was able to finish the review test yesterday.

### D. Jason Quick Check / Jason 快速檢查
EN: Ask whether you mean present ability, general past ability, or one successful past action.

中：先問自己是「現在能力」、「過去一般能力」，還是「過去某一次成功做到」。

- can = now
- could = general past
- was/were able to = succeeded once

## Examples
- I can review Unit 43 today.
- I could understand simple examples before.
- I was able to correct the sentence after checking the rule.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- EN: **Could** = general past ability.
- 中：**could** 比較像「以前有這個能力」。
- EN: **Was able to** = succeeded in one past situation.
- 中：**was able to** 比較像「那一次成功做到」。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I could finish the test yesterday.
- Better: I was able to finish the test yesterday.
- Wrong: I can to understand it.
- Correct: I can understand it.

## One-line Summary
EN: Use **could** for general past ability and **was able to** for one successful past action.

中：過去一般能力用 **could**；某次成功做到用 **was able to**。
