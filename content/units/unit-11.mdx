## Learning Goal
EN: Ask and answer about duration until now.

中：學會詢問和回答持續到現在多久。

## Core Grammar
### A. How Long / 多久
EN: Use how long for duration.

中：how long 問持續多久。

### B. Have Been Doing / 一直在做
EN: Use have been doing for ongoing activities.

中：持續活動常用 have been doing。

### C. Have Known / 狀態動詞
EN: Some state verbs use present perfect simple.

中：狀態動詞常用現在完成式。

### D. Jason Quick Check / Jason 快速檢查
EN: Before answering, identify the time, verb form, and sentence meaning.

中：作答前先判斷時間、動詞形式和句子意思，不要只靠中文直翻。

## Examples
- How long have you been studying grammar?
- I have been studying for two hours.
- How long have you known this rule?

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Use present perfect forms for duration continuing to now.
- 中：持續到現在的時間長度常用完成式。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: How long do you study grammar?
- Better: How long have you been studying grammar?

## One-line Summary
EN: How long + present perfect asks duration until now.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
