## Learning Goal
EN: Review advanced passive patterns and avoid passive forms that sound incomplete.

中：複習進階被動句型，避免寫出缺少 **be / been / being** 的不完整被動。

## Core Grammar
### A. Passive With Two Objects / 雙受詞被動
EN: Some verbs can have two objects, and either object can become the passive subject.

中：有些動詞有兩個受詞，兩個受詞都有可能變成被動句主詞。

- Active: They gave Jason an explanation.
- Passive: Jason was given an explanation.
- Passive: An explanation was given to Jason.

### B. Passive With Reporting Or Thinking / 被動接觀點
EN: Passive often appears when the speaker does not want to focus on who says or believes something.

中：當不想強調「誰說的/誰認為的」，英文常用被動。

- This pattern is considered important.
- The answer is believed to be correct.

### C. Passive + Infinitive / 被動 + to
EN: Some passive structures are followed by **to + base verb**.

中：有些被動結構後面會接 **to + 原形動詞**。

- Jason was asked to review the examples.
- The rule is expected to be useful.

### D. Jason Warning / Jason 注意
EN: If the sentence is passive, do not forget the form of **be**.

中：只要是被動，不要忘記 **be** 的某種形式。

## Examples
- Jason was given a clearer explanation.
- The wrong answer was added to the review list.
- The sentence was expected to be corrected.
- This pattern is considered useful for Unit 45.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- EN: Passive can appear before another verb pattern, such as **was asked to review**.
- 中：被動後面還可以接另一個動詞結構，例如 **was asked to review**。
- EN: When two passive versions are possible, choose the one that sounds clearer.
- 中：雙受詞被動有時有兩種寫法，選比較清楚的那一個。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: Jason gave an explanation. (when Jason received it)
- Correct: Jason was given an explanation.
- Wrong: The rule expected to be useful.
- Correct: The rule is expected to be useful.

## One-line Summary
EN: Passive can combine with other structures, but it still needs **be + past participle**.

中：被動可以接其他句型，但核心仍然是 **be + 過去分詞**。
