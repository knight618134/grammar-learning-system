## Learning Goal
EN: Use **if + past** and **wish + past** for unreal present situations.

中：學會用 **if + 過去式** 和 **wish + 過去式** 表達「現在不是這樣，但希望是這樣」。

## Core Grammar
### A. If I knew / 如果我知道
EN: **If + past, would/could/might + base verb** describes an unreal present or future.

中：**if + 過去式, would/could/might + 原形動詞** 表達現在或未來的假設。

- If I knew the answer, I would explain it.
- If I had more time, I could review again.

### B. I wish I knew / 我希望我知道
EN: **Wish + past** means the real present situation is different from what you want.

中：**wish + 過去式** 表示「現在事實不是這樣，但我希望是這樣」。

- I wish I knew the answer. = I do not know the answer now.
- I wish I had more time. = I do not have enough time now.

### C. Wish Is Not Hope / wish 不是 hope
EN: Use **hope** for possible future outcomes. Use **wish** for unreal or regretful situations.

中：**hope** 是希望未來可能發生；**wish** 常常是「現在不是真的」或「後悔」。

- I hope I improve soon. (possible)
- I wish I understood this now. (not true now)

### D. Was / Were After Wish / wish 後的 was/were
EN: In formal English, **I wish I were...** is common for unreal situations. In everyday English, people also say **I wish I was...**.

中：正式一點常用 **I wish I were...** 表達假設；口語也常聽到 **I wish I was...**。

- I wish I were more confident.
- If I were you, I would review the examples again.

## Examples
- If I knew the answer, I would explain it.
- I wish I knew why this sentence uses passive.
- If I had more time, I could review Unit 42 again.
- I wish I understood the difference between **be done** and **been done**.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- EN: **I wish I had...** can mean "I do not have it now."
- 中：**I wish I had...** 很常表示「我現在沒有」。
- EN: The past form after **wish** often describes an unreal present.
- 中：wish 後面的過去式常常不是過去，而是「現在假設」。
- EN: Do not use **will** right after **wish** for your own future improvement. Use **hope**.
- 中：說自己未來會進步，用 **hope** 比 **wish** 自然。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I wish I have more time.
- Correct: I wish I had more time.
- Wrong: If I know the answer, I would tell you.
- Correct: If I knew the answer, I would tell you.
- Wrong: I wish I will understand it tomorrow.
- Better: I hope I will understand it tomorrow.

## One-line Summary
EN: Use **wish + past** for something you want to be different now.

中：**wish + 過去式** 表達「我希望現在不是這樣」。
