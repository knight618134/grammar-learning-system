## Learning Goal
EN: Use third conditionals and **wish + past perfect** for unreal past situations.

中：學會用第三條件句和 **wish + had done** 表達「過去如果不同就好了」。

## Core Grammar
### A. Third Conditional / 第三條件句
EN: Use **if + had + past participle, would have + past participle** for an unreal past condition and result.

中：用 **if + had + 過去分詞, would have + 過去分詞** 表達過去沒有發生的假設。

- If I had checked the tense, I would have answered correctly.
- If I had reviewed Passive 1, I would have understood Passive 2 faster.

### B. Wish + Past Perfect / wish + had done
EN: Use **wish + had + past participle** for regret about the past.

中：用 **wish + had + 過去分詞** 表達對過去的後悔。

- I wish I had checked the rule earlier.
- I wish I had noticed the passive form.

### C. Would Have Belongs In The Result / would have 放結果句
EN: In the standard third conditional, do not put **would have** in the if-clause.

中：標準第三條件句裡，**would have** 放在結果句，不放 if 子句。

- Wrong: If I would have known, I would have answered.
- Correct: If I had known, I would have answered.

### D. Present Regret vs Past Regret / 現在遺憾 vs 過去後悔
EN: **Wish + past** is unreal present. **Wish + past perfect** is past regret.

中：**wish + 過去式** 是現在不是真的；**wish + had done** 是過去沒做到。

- I wish I knew. = I do not know now.
- I wish I had known. = I did not know then.

## Examples
- If I had checked the tense, I would have answered correctly.
- I wish I had reviewed Passive 1 before Passive 2.
- If I had known the rule, I wouldn't have chosen that answer.
- I wish I had saved that ChatGPT explanation into my notes.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- EN: **Had known** marks an unreal past condition.
- 中：**had known** 在這裡表示過去假設，不只是普通過去完成式。
- EN: **Would have** belongs in the result clause.
- 中：**would have** 放在結果句，不要塞到 if 後面。
- EN: If the regret is about yesterday, last week, or a previous answer, use **wish + had done**.
- 中：如果後悔的是昨天、上週、之前的答案，用 **wish + had done**。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: If I would have known, I would have answered.
- Correct: If I had known, I would have answered.
- Wrong: I wish I knew it yesterday.
- Better: I wish I had known it yesterday.
- Wrong: If I checked it, I would have answered correctly.
- Better: If I had checked it, I would have answered correctly.

## One-line Summary
EN: Use **if/wish + had done** for unreal or regretted past situations.

中：過去沒發生、現在後悔，用 **if/wish + had done**。
