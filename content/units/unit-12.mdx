## Learning Goal
EN: Use **for** and **since** with duration.

中：學會用 **for** 和 **since** 表達持續時間。

## Core Grammar
### A. For / 一段時間
EN: Use for + length of time.

中：for + 一段時間。

### B. Since / 起點
EN: Use since + starting point.

中：since + 起始時間。

### C. How Long / 問多久
EN: Use how long to ask about duration.

中：用 how long 問持續多久。

## Examples
- I have studied for two hours.
- I have studied since 8 p.m.
- How long have you used this app?

## Jason's Confusing Points
- For is length; since is starting point.
- 中：for 是多久；since 是從何時開始。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I have studied since two hours.
- Correct: I have studied for two hours.

## One-line Summary
EN: For = duration; since = starting point.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
