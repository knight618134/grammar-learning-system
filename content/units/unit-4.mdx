## Learning Goal
EN: Understand verbs that are usually not used in continuous form.

中：理解有些狀態動詞通常不用進行式。

## Core Grammar
### A. State Verbs / 狀態動詞
EN: Verbs like know, believe, understand often describe states.

中：know, believe, understand 常表示狀態。

### B. Action Verbs / 動作動詞
EN: Action verbs can often use continuous.

中：動作動詞通常可以用進行式。

### C. Meaning Change / 意思改變
EN: Some verbs change meaning in continuous form.

中：有些動詞用進行式時意思會變。

## Examples
- I understand the rule now.
- I am thinking about the answer.
- I know this pattern.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Say I understand, not I am understanding in normal use.
- 中：一般說 I understand，不說 I am understanding。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I am knowing the answer.
- Correct: I know the answer.

## One-line Summary
EN: Use simple for many state verbs; continuous for actions.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
