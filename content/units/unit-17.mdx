## Learning Goal
EN: Use **have** and **have got** for possession and related meanings.

中：學會用 **have / have got** 表達擁有、關係、身體狀態等。

## Core Grammar
### A. Possession / 擁有
EN: Have and have got can both mean possess.

中：have 和 have got 都可表示擁有。

### B. Questions / 問句
EN: Use do/does with have; use have/has with have got.

中：have 問句常用 do/does；have got 用 have/has。

### C. American/British / 語感差異
EN: Have got is especially common in British English.

中：have got 在英式英文很常見。

### D. Jason Quick Check / Jason 快速檢查
EN: Before answering, identify the time, verb form, and sentence meaning.

中：作答前先判斷時間、動詞形式和句子意思，不要只靠中文直翻。

## Examples
- I have a grammar notebook.
- I've got a question.
- Jason has a review plan.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Do not mix forms: Do you have...? / Have you got...?
- 中：不要混：Do you have...? / Have you got...?

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: Do you have got a question?
- Correct: Do you have a question?

## One-line Summary
EN: Have and have got can both show possession, but their question forms differ.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
