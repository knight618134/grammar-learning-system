## Learning Goal
EN: Use past simple for completed actions in the past.

中：用過去簡單式表達過去已完成的動作。

## Core Grammar
### A. Finished Past / 已完成的過去
EN: Use past simple for actions completed at a past time.

中：過去某時間完成的事用過去簡單式。

### B. Time Markers / 時間標記
EN: yesterday, last week, in 2020 often suggest past simple.

中：yesterday, last week, in 2020 常提示過去式。

### C. Did Questions / did 問句
EN: Use did + base verb in questions and negatives.

中：問句和否定用 did + 原形。

## Examples
- I reviewed Unit 5 yesterday.
- Jason made a mistake.
- Did you check the answer?

## Jason's Confusing Points
- After did, use base verb.
- 中：did 後面接原形動詞。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: Did you checked it?
- Correct: Did you check it?

## One-line Summary
EN: Past simple = completed past action.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
