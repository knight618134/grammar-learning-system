title: I'd better ... it's time ...
---

## Learning Goal
EN: Use **had better** for urgent advice and **it's time** when something should happen now.

中：學會用 **had better** 表達強烈建議，用 **it's time** 表達「該做了」。

## Core Grammar
### A. Had Better / 最好趕快
EN: **Had better + base verb** gives strong advice. It often suggests there may be a problem if you do not do it.

中：**had better + 原形動詞** 是比較強的建議，常有「不做可能會有問題」的感覺。

- You'd better save your notes.
- You'd better review Unit 43 again.

### B. Had Better Not / 最好不要
EN: Negative form is **had better not + base verb**.

中：否定是 **had better not + 原形動詞**。

- You'd better not ignore this mistake.

### C. It's Time + Past / 該做了
EN: Use **it's time + subject + past verb** when something should happen now or is already late.

中：用 **it's time + 主詞 + 過去式** 表達「現在該做了，甚至有點晚了」。

- It's time you reviewed the wrong answers.

### D. No To After Better / better 後不加 to
EN: Do not use **to** after **had better**.

中：**had better** 後面直接接原形動詞，不加 **to**。

## Examples
- You'd better save your notes before editing.
- We had better not ignore this mistake.
- It's time you reviewed Unit 42 again.
- It's time Jason turned ChatGPT explanations into notes.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- EN: **Had better** is stronger than **should**.
- 中：**had better** 比 **should** 更急、更強。
- EN: After **it's time**, the verb looks past but means now.
- 中：**it's time** 後面動詞看起來是過去式，但意思是現在該做。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: You had better to study.
- Correct: You had better study.
- Wrong: It's time you review this unit.
- Correct: It's time you reviewed this unit.

## One-line Summary
EN: **Had better** is urgent advice; **it's time + past** means something is overdue.

中：**had better** 是強烈建議；**it's time + 過去式** 表示該做了。
