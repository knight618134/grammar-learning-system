## Learning Goal
EN: Use present continuous for actions happening now or around now.

中：學會用現在進行式表達正在發生、或最近一段時間正在進行的事。

## Core Grammar
### A. Form / 形式
EN: am/is/are + -ing

中：be 動詞 + V-ing

### B. Now / 現在正在
EN: Use it for actions happening at the moment.

中：用來說現在正在做的事。

### C. Around now / 最近一段時間
EN: Use it for temporary situations around now.

中：用來說最近暫時正在進行的狀態。

### D. Jason Quick Check / Jason 快速檢查
EN: Before answering, identify the time, verb form, and sentence meaning.

中：作答前先判斷時間、動詞形式和句子意思，不要只靠中文直翻。

## Examples
- I am reviewing Unit 1 now.
- Jason is building a grammar system.
- This app is becoming more useful.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Do not forget am/is/are.
- 中：不要只寫 V-ing，前面要有 am/is/are。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I reviewing grammar.
- Correct: I am reviewing grammar.

## One-line Summary
EN: Present continuous = am/is/are + -ing for now or around now.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
