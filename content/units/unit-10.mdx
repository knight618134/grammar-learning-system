title: Present perfect continuous and simple (I have been doing and I have done)
---

## Learning Goal
EN: Choose between activity focus and result focus.

中：分辨現在完成進行式的活動焦點，和現在完成式的結果焦點。

## Core Grammar
### A. Activity / 活動
EN: Have been doing emphasizes the activity or duration.

中：have been doing 強調活動或持續。

### B. Result / 結果
EN: Have done emphasizes completion or result.

中：have done 強調完成或結果。

### C. Both Can Be Possible / 兩者都可能
EN: Choose by what you want to emphasize.

中：依照你想強調活動還是結果來選。

## Examples
- I have been writing notes.
- I have written three unit notes.
- Jason has been studying, and he has improved.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Ask: activity or finished result?
- 中：先問自己要強調活動，還是完成結果。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I have been finished the note.
- Correct: I have finished the note.

## One-line Summary
EN: Have been doing = activity; have done = result.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
