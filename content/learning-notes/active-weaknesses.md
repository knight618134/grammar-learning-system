# Active Weaknesses

## Passive

Current focus:

- Unit 42: basic passive form, tense carried by **be**
- Unit 43: **be done**, **been done**, **being done**

Memory hooks:

- Passive is not just a past participle. It needs a form of **be**.
- After modal verbs, use **modal + be + past participle**.
- In perfect passive, use **have/has/had been + past participle**.
- In continuous passive, use **be being + past participle**.

## Wish

Current focus:

- Unit 39: **wish + past** for unreal present
- Unit 40: **wish + past perfect** for past regret
- Unit 41: broader wish patterns

Memory hooks:

- **I wish I had more time** means I do not have enough time now.
- **I wish I had known** means I did not know in the past.
- Use **hope**, not **wish**, for possible future outcomes.

## Conditionals

Current focus:

- Unit 38: real vs unreal condition
- Unit 39: unreal present condition
- Unit 40: unreal past condition

Memory hooks:

- Real future: **If I study, I will improve**.
- Unreal present: **If I had time, I would review**.
- Unreal past: **If I had known, I would have answered correctly**.

## Modal Verbs

Current focus:

- Unit 26-32: ability, deduction, possibility, obligation
- Unit 33-37: should, had better, would, requests, offers, permission

Memory hooks:

- **Mustn't** means prohibition.
- **Don't have to** means no obligation.
- **Can't** can mean negative deduction.
- **Should have done** means the better action was in the past.
