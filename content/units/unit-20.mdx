## Learning Goal
EN: Use **be going to** for intentions and evidence-based predictions.

中：用 **be going to** 表達打算，以及根據跡象做出的預測。

## Core Grammar
### A. Intention / 打算
EN: Use going to for plans or intentions.

中：用 going to 表達已經有的打算。

### B. Evidence / 有跡象
EN: Use going to when something seems likely from evidence.

中：根據眼前跡象判斷會發生。

### C. Form / 形式
EN: am/is/are going to + base verb

中：am/is/are going to + 原形動詞。

### D. Jason Quick Check / Jason 快速檢查
EN: Before answering, identify the time, verb form, and sentence meaning.

中：作答前先判斷時間、動詞形式和句子意思，不要只靠中文直翻。

## Examples
- I am going to review Unit 43.
- This mistake is going to appear again if I ignore it.
- Jason is going to add notes.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Going to needs be: am/is/are.
- 中：going to 前面要有 be。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I going to study.
- Correct: I am going to study.

## One-line Summary
EN: Going to = intention or prediction from evidence.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
