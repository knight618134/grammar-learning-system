## Learning Goal
EN: Use present perfect for new information and past simple for details.

中：用現在完成式引入新資訊，再用過去式說細節。

## Core Grammar
### A. New Information / 新資訊
EN: Present perfect often introduces news or experience.

中：現在完成式常用來帶出新資訊或經驗。

### B. Details / 細節
EN: Past simple often gives when/how details.

中：過去式常用來說何時、如何發生。

### C. Conversation Flow / 對話流程
EN: Start with have done, continue with did.

中：先用 have done，引出後再用 did 補細節。

### D. Jason Quick Check / Jason 快速檢查
EN: Before answering, identify the time, verb form, and sentence meaning.

中：作答前先判斷時間、動詞形式和句子意思，不要只靠中文直翻。

## Examples
- I have made a mistake. I chose the wrong tense.
- Jason has updated the notes. He changed Unit 43 yesterday.
- I have read this rule before.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- English often switches tense after the first sentence.
- 中：英文常先用完成式，再切到過去式說細節。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I have made a mistake yesterday.
- Correct: I made a mistake yesterday.

## One-line Summary
EN: Use present perfect for news, past simple for past details.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
