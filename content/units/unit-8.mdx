## Learning Goal
EN: Use present perfect for experience and unfinished time.

中：用現在完成式表達經驗和還沒結束的時間範圍。

## Core Grammar
### A. Experience / 經驗
EN: Use it for life experience without saying exactly when.

中：不說確切時間的經驗用現在完成式。

### B. Unfinished Time / 未結束時間
EN: Use it with today, this week, this year when the period is not finished.

中：today, this week 等未結束時間可用現在完成式。

### C. Ever/Never / 曾經/從未
EN: Ever and never are common with present perfect.

中：ever/never 常和現在完成式一起用。

## Examples
- I have studied passive before.
- Jason has made progress this week.
- Have you ever confused been and being?

## Jason's Confusing Points
- If you say exactly when, past simple is often better.
- 中：如果說明確時間，通常用過去簡單式。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I have studied it last night.
- Correct: I studied it last night.

## One-line Summary
EN: Use present perfect for experience and unfinished time.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
