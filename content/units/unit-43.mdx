title: It is said that / He is said to
---

## Learning Goal
Use reporting passive structures to report claims, beliefs, and public information.

## Core Grammar
Two common patterns are **It is said that + clause** and **subject + is said to + base verb**. If the reported action happened earlier, use **subject + is said to have + past participle**.

## Examples
- It is said that he is a careful learner.
- He is said to study every night.
- She is said to have passed the exam last year.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- **Is said to do** means the action is at the same time or generally true.
- **Is said to have done** means the reported action happened earlier.

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: He is said that he studies hard.
- Correct: It is said that he studies hard.
- Correct: He is said to study hard.
- Wrong: He is said to left the company in 2020.
- Correct: He is said to have left the company in 2020.

## One-line Summary
Use **It is said that...** or **is said to...**, and use **to have done** for earlier actions.
