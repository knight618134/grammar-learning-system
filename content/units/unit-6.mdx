## Learning Goal
EN: Use past continuous for actions in progress at a past time.

中：用過去進行式表達過去某時正在發生的動作。

## Core Grammar
### A. Form / 形式
EN: was/were + -ing

中：was/were + V-ing

### B. In Progress / 正在進行
EN: Use it for an action continuing at a past moment.

中：用來說過去某一刻正在進行。

### C. Background Action / 背景動作
EN: It often gives background for another past action.

中：常用來當另一個過去動作的背景。

### D. Jason Quick Check / Jason 快速檢查
EN: Before answering, identify the time, verb form, and sentence meaning.

中：作答前先判斷時間、動詞形式和句子意思，不要只靠中文直翻。

## Examples
- I was reviewing when you called.
- Jason was checking the passive form.
- The app was running locally.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Past continuous is about duration or background.
- 中：過去進行式常強調當時正在進行。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I was review when you called.
- Correct: I was reviewing when you called.

## One-line Summary
EN: Past continuous = was/were + -ing for past action in progress.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
