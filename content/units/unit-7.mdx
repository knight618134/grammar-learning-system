## Learning Goal
EN: Use present perfect for past actions connected to now.

中：用現在完成式表達和現在有關聯的過去動作。

## Core Grammar
### A. Form / 形式
EN: have/has + past participle

中：have/has + 過去分詞

### B. Connection To Now / 連到現在
EN: The result or experience matters now.

中：重點是結果或經驗和現在有關。

### C. No Finished Time / 不說明確過去時間
EN: Do not use it with finished time markers like yesterday.

中：不要和 yesterday 這種明確過去時間一起用。

## Examples
- I have finished Unit 7.
- Jason has saved the notes.
- Have you checked the answer?

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Present perfect is not the same as past simple.
- 中：現在完成式不是單純過去式。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I have finished it yesterday.
- Correct: I finished it yesterday.

## One-line Summary
EN: Present perfect = past action with present connection.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
