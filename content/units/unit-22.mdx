## Learning Goal
EN: Use **will** and **shall** in questions, offers, and suggestions.

中：學會在問句、提議、提供幫忙中使用 will / shall。

## Core Grammar
### A. Will You...? / 請求
EN: Use will you for requests or willingness.

中：will you 可問對方願不願意。

### B. Shall I...? / 我來...好嗎
EN: Use shall I for offers.

中：shall I 可用來主動提供幫忙。

### C. Shall We...? / 我們...好嗎
EN: Use shall we for suggestions.

中：shall we 可用來提議一起做。

## Examples
- Will you check this answer?
- Shall I add this to wrong answers?
- Shall we review Passive first?

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Shall is common in offers/suggestions, especially with I/we.
- 中：shall 常和 I/we 搭配，用於提議或提供。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: Shall you review this?
- Better: Will you review this?

## One-line Summary
EN: Shall I/we is useful for offers and suggestions.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
