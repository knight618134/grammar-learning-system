title: Had better / It's time
---

## Learning Goal
Use **had better** for urgent advice and **It's time** to say something should happen now.

## Core Grammar
**Had better + base verb** means there may be a problem if the action is not taken. **It's time + subject + past verb** describes a present situation that is already late.

## Examples
- You had better save your notes.
- We had better not ignore this mistake.
- It's time you reviewed Unit 42 again.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- **Had better** is stronger than **should**.
- After **It's time**, the verb often looks past but means now.

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: You had better to study.
- Correct: You had better study.
- Wrong: It's time you review this unit.
- Correct: It's time you reviewed this unit.

## One-line Summary
Use **had better** for strong advice and **It's time + past** for something overdue.
