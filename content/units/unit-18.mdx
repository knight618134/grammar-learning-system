## Learning Goal
EN: Use **used to** for past habits or past states that are no longer true.

中：用 **used to** 表達過去習慣或過去狀態，現在已經不同。

## Core Grammar
### A. Past Habit / 過去習慣
EN: Used to describes repeated past actions.

中：used to 可描述過去常做的事。

### B. Past State / 過去狀態
EN: Used to can describe past states.

中：used to 也可描述過去狀態。

### C. No Longer True / 現在不同
EN: It often suggests the situation changed.

中：通常表示現在已經不同。

## Examples
- I used to avoid grammar.
- Jason used to confuse wish and hope.
- This used to be difficult.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Use base verb after used to.
- 中：used to 後面接原形動詞。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I used to studied grammar.
- Correct: I used to study grammar.

## One-line Summary
EN: Used to = past habit/state that is not true now.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
