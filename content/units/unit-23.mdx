## Learning Goal
EN: Choose between **will** and **going to**.

中：分辨 **will** 和 **going to** 的使用時機。

## Core Grammar
### A. Will / 當下決定
EN: Use will for decisions made now.

中：當下決定用 will。

### B. Going To / 已有計畫
EN: Use going to for intentions or plans already made.

中：已有打算或計畫用 going to。

### C. Prediction / 預測
EN: Both can predict; going to often uses visible evidence.

中：兩者都可預測；going to 常有眼前跡象。

## Examples
- I will fix it now.
- I am going to review Unit 24 tonight.
- Look at the errors. This is going to need review.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Ask: decided now or planned before?
- 中：先問是當下決定，還是之前就計畫好。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I am going fix it.
- Correct: I am going to fix it.

## One-line Summary
EN: Will = decision now; going to = plan or evidence.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
