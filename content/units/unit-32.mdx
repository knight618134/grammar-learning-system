## Learning Goal
EN: Distinguish **must**, **mustn't**, **needn't**, and **don't have to**.

中：分辨 **must**、**mustn't**、**needn't**、**don't have to**，尤其是「禁止」和「不必」。

## Core Grammar
### A. Must / 必須
EN: **Must** means something is necessary or strongly required.

中：**must** 表示必須。

- You must check the tense.

### B. Mustn't / 禁止
EN: **Mustn't** means do not do it. It is prohibition.

中：**mustn't** 是禁止，不是「不必」。

- You mustn't smoke here.

### C. Needn't / Don't Have To / 不必
EN: **Needn't** and **don't have to** mean it is not necessary.

中：**needn't** 和 **don't have to** 表示不需要、不必。

- You don't have to memorize every sentence.
- You needn't review Unit 1 today.

### D. Jason Warning / Jason 注意
EN: **Mustn't** and **don't have to** are very different.

中：**mustn't** = 禁止；**don't have to** = 不必。

## Examples
- You must review Unit 43.
- You mustn't ignore **be** in passive sentences.
- You don't have to copy the textbook.
- You needn't finish all 145 units now.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- EN: **Mustn't** does not mean "not necessary".
- 中：**mustn't** 不是不必，而是不能。
- EN: **Don't have to** does not mean prohibition.
- 中：**don't have to** 不是禁止，只是不需要。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: You don't have to smoke here. It is forbidden.
- Correct: You mustn't smoke here. It is forbidden.
- Wrong: You mustn't memorize every example.
- Better: You don't have to memorize every example.

## One-line Summary
EN: **Mustn't** = prohibited. **Don't have to / needn't** = not necessary.

中：**mustn't** = 禁止；**don't have to / needn't** = 不必。
