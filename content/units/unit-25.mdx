## Learning Goal
EN: Use present forms after when/if for future meaning.

中：學會在 when/if 後面用現在式表達未來。

## Core Grammar
### A. After When/If / when/if 後
EN: Use present simple for future meaning after when and if.

中：when/if 後用現在式表達未來。

### B. When I've Done / 做完之後
EN: Use present perfect for completion before a future point.

中：用現在完成式表示未來某事之前已完成。

### C. If vs When / 如果 vs 當
EN: If means uncertain; when means expected.

中：if 是不確定；when 是預期會發生。

## Examples
- When I finish Unit 25, I will review Unit 26.
- If I have time, I will practice.
- When I have finished this note, I will push it.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Do not use will right after when/if in basic future time clauses.
- 中：基本未來時間/條件子句裡，when/if 後不要直接用 will。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: When I will finish, I will review.
- Correct: When I finish, I will review.

## One-line Summary
