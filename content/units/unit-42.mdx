## Learning Goal
EN: Build basic passive sentences when the action or result matters more than the doer.

中：學會在「動作或結果比執行者更重要」時使用被動語態。

## Core Grammar
### A. Active vs Passive / 主動 vs 被動
EN: Active focuses on the person or thing doing the action. Passive focuses on the person or thing receiving the action.

中：主動句強調「誰做了動作」；被動句強調「誰/什麼承受了動作」。

- Active: Someone corrected the mistake.
- Passive: The mistake was corrected.

### B. Passive Form / 被動基本公式
EN: Passive = **be + past participle**. The tense is shown by **be**, not by the past participle.

中：被動語態 = **be 動詞 + 過去分詞**。時態看 **be 動詞**，不是看過去分詞。

- Present: The room **is cleaned** every day.
- Past: The room **was cleaned** yesterday.
- Future: The room **will be cleaned** tomorrow.

### C. When to Use Passive / 什麼時候用被動
EN: Use passive when the doer is unknown, unimportant, obvious, or when the result is more important.

中：當「誰做的」不知道、不重要、很明顯，或你想強調結果時，就常用被動。

- My phone was stolen. (I do not know who stole it.)
- The test was marked. (The result matters.)

### D. By + Agent / by + 執行者
EN: Use **by + person/thing** only when the doer is useful information.

中：只有在「誰做的」很重要時，才加 **by + 人/物**。

- The book was written by Raymond Murphy.
- The window was broken. (No need to say by someone if it is unknown or unimportant.)

## Examples
- The mistake was corrected.
- These examples are used often.
- The lesson will be reviewed tomorrow.
- The sentence was changed because the grammar was wrong.
- This unit is connected to Unit 43.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- The tense is carried by **be**, not by the past participle.
- Do not add **by someone** unless it helps.
- 中：不要看到過去分詞就以為是過去式。被動的時間要看 **is / was / will be / has been**。
- 中：中文常常不說「被」，但英文句子需要明確的被動結構。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: The answer corrected by Jason.
- Correct: The answer was corrected by Jason.
- Wrong: The test is finish.
- Correct: The test is finished.
- Wrong: The mistake was correct.
- Correct: The mistake was corrected.

## One-line Summary
EN: Passive = **be + past participle**, with the tense shown in **be**.

中：被動語態就是 **be + 過去分詞**，時態放在 **be** 上。
