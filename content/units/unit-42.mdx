## Learning Goal
EN: Build basic passive sentences when the action, result, or receiver is more important than the doer.

中：學會在「動作、結果、承受者」比「誰做的」更重要時，使用基本被動語態。

## Core Grammar
### A. Active vs Passive / 主動 vs 被動
EN: Active sentences focus on the doer. Passive sentences focus on the receiver of the action.

中：主動句重點是「誰做了動作」；被動句重點是「誰/什麼承受了動作」。

- Active: Someone corrected the mistake.
- Passive: The mistake was corrected.
- Active: People use this pattern often.
- Passive: This pattern is used often.

### B. Passive Form / 被動基本公式
EN: Passive = **be + past participle**. The tense is shown by **be**.

中：被動語態 = **be 動詞 + 過去分詞**。時態放在 **be 動詞** 上。

- Present simple passive: The room **is cleaned** every day.
- Past simple passive: The room **was cleaned** yesterday.
- Future passive: The room **will be cleaned** tomorrow.

### C. When Passive Is Natural / 什麼時候自然用被動
EN: Passive is common when the doer is unknown, unimportant, obvious, or less important than the result.

中：當「誰做的」不知道、不重要、很明顯，或你想強調結果時，英文常用被動。

- My phone was stolen. (I do not know who stole it.)
- The test was marked. (The result matters.)
- English is spoken in many countries. (The speakers are not the focus.)

### D. By + Agent / by + 執行者
EN: Add **by + doer** only when the doer is useful or important information.

中：只有當「誰做的」真的重要，才加 **by + 人/物**。

- The book was written by Raymond Murphy.
- The window was broken. (No need to add "by someone".)

### E. Tense Check / 時態檢查法
EN: To check passive, ask two questions: Where is **be**? What tense is **be**?

中：檢查被動句時問兩件事：有沒有 **be**？**be** 是什麼時態？

- The answer **is** corrected. = present passive
- The answer **was** corrected. = past passive
- The answer **will be** corrected. = future passive

## Examples
- The mistake was corrected.
- These examples are used often.
- The lesson will be reviewed tomorrow.
- The sentence was changed because the grammar was wrong.
- This unit is connected to Unit 43.
- The wrong answer was saved for review.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- EN: Do not write only the past participle. Passive needs **be**.
- 中：不要只寫過去分詞。被動一定要有 **be**。
- EN: The past participle does not show the tense by itself.
- 中：過去分詞本身不負責時態；時態看 **is / was / will be**。
- EN: Chinese often omits the passive marker, but English still needs the passive structure.
- 中：中文可能不說「被」，但英文句子仍然需要明確的被動結構。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: The answer corrected by Jason.
- Correct: The answer was corrected by Jason.
- Wrong: The test is finish.
- Correct: The test is finished.
- Wrong: The mistake was correct.
- Correct: The mistake was corrected.
- Wrong: This rule uses often.
- Correct: This rule is used often.

## One-line Summary
EN: Passive = **be + past participle**, and the tense lives in **be**.

中：被動語態就是 **be + 過去分詞**，時態放在 **be** 上。
