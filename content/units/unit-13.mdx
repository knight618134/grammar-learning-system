## Learning Goal
EN: Choose between present perfect and past simple.

中：分辨現在完成式和過去簡單式。

## Core Grammar
### A. Present Perfect / 現在完成
EN: Use it when the result or experience matters now.

中：結果或經驗和現在有關。

### B. Past Simple / 過去簡單
EN: Use it with a finished past time.

中：明確過去時間用過去簡單式。

### C. Time Mentioned / 有說時間
EN: If you say when, past simple is usually right.

中：有說何時，通常用過去式。

## Examples
- I have finished the note.
- I finished it yesterday.
- Jason has learned the pattern.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Do not use present perfect with finished past time.
- 中：不要把現在完成式和明確過去時間混在一起。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I have finished it yesterday.
- Correct: I finished it yesterday.

## One-line Summary
EN: Have done connects to now; did belongs to finished past time.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
