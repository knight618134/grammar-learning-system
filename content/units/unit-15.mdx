## Learning Goal
EN: Use past perfect for an action before another past time.

中：用過去完成式表達比另一個過去時間更早發生的事。

## Core Grammar
### A. Earlier Past / 更早的過去
EN: Use had done for an action before another past action.

中：比另一個過去動作更早，用 had done。

### B. Form / 形式
EN: had + past participle

中：had + 過去分詞

### C. Clarity / 清楚順序
EN: It helps show which past action happened first.

中：幫助說清楚過去事件順序。

### D. Jason Quick Check / Jason 快速檢查
EN: Before answering, identify the time, verb form, and sentence meaning.

中：作答前先判斷時間、動詞形式和句子意思，不要只靠中文直翻。

## Examples
- I had reviewed Unit 42 before I studied Unit 43.
- Jason had saved the note before the app restarted.
- The answer had been corrected.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Past perfect is about past-before-past.
- 中：過去完成式是過去中的更早過去。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: I had review it before.
- Correct: I had reviewed it before.

## One-line Summary
EN: Past perfect = had done for earlier past.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
