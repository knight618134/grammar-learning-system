## Learning Goal
EN: Use present tenses for future arrangements and schedules.

中：學會用現在式表達未來安排和固定時程。

## Core Grammar
### A. Present Continuous / 未來安排
EN: Use am/is/are doing for personal arrangements.

中：用現在進行式表達已安排好的未來計畫。

### B. Present Simple / 固定時程
EN: Use present simple for timetables and schedules.

中：用現在簡單式表達固定時刻表。

### C. Context / 看語境
EN: Future meaning comes from time words or context.

中：未來意思常由時間字或語境看出。

### D. Jason Quick Check / Jason 快速檢查
EN: Before answering, identify the time, verb form, and sentence meaning.

中：作答前先判斷時間、動詞形式和句子意思，不要只靠中文直翻。

## Examples
- I am reviewing Unit 20 tomorrow.
- The class starts at 7.
- Jason is meeting a tutor next week.

## Jason's Confusing Points
- Present form can have future meaning.
- 中：現在式也可以表達未來。

## Wrong Answers
- Wrong: The class is start at 7.
- Correct: The class starts at 7.

## One-line Summary
EN: Present continuous = arrangement; present simple = schedule.

中：本單元重點請看上方公式、例句和常錯點；複習時先確認時態和動詞形式。
